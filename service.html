Aurora Café Recipes

Latte Recipe

Learn how to make the perfect latte with a shot of espresso, steamed milk, and a touch of foam.

  • Ingredients: Espresso, Steamed Milk, Foam
  • Steps:
    1. Brew a shot of espresso.
    2. Steam the milk until frothy.
    3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, leaving a bit of foam to top it off.

Chicken Wrap Recipe

A delicious and healthy wrap with grilled chicken, fresh vegetables, and a tangy sauce.

  • Ingredients: Grilled Chicken, Tortilla, Lettuce, Tomato, Sauce
  • Steps:
    1. Grill the chicken and slice it into strips.
    2. Layer the chicken on a tortilla with lettuce, tomato, and your favorite sauce.
    3. Wrap it up tightly and serve!

Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

  • Ingredients: Spaghetti, Eggs, Parmesan Cheese, Beef, Black Pepper
  • Steps:
    1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
    2. Fry the beef until crispy, then mix with beaten eggs and cheese.
    3. Combine the pasta with the beef mixture, adding pepper to taste.

Fruit Punch Recipe

A refreshing and fruity drink perfect for any occasion.

  • Ingredients: Orange Juice, Pineapple Juice, Lemon-Lime Soda, Fresh Fruits (e.g., Strawberries, Orange Slices)
  • Steps:
    1. Mix orange juice and pineapple juice in a large pitcher.
    2. Add lemon-lime soda for a fizzy touch.
    3. Stir in fresh fruits and serve chilled.

Fruit Tart Recipe

A delightful dessert with a buttery crust, creamy filling, and fresh fruit topping.

  • Ingredients: Tart Crust, Cream Cheese Filling, Fresh Fruits (e.g., Berries, Kiwi)
  • Steps:
    1. Prepare the tart crust and bake it until golden brown.
    2. Whip cream cheese with sugar and spread it over the crust.
    3. Top with fresh fruits and chill before serving.

Kimchi Jjigae Recipe

A traditional Korean stew made with kimchi, tofu, and pork or seafood.

  • Ingredients: Kimchi, Tofu, Seafood, Garlic, Gochugaru (Korean Red Pepper Flakes)
  • Steps:
    1. Sauté kimchi and garlic in a pot.
    2. Add seafood and water, then bring to a boil.
    3. Add tofu and simmer until flavors meld together.
