Chef Alain Ducasse

Experience: Over 30 Michelin stars across his career, making him one of the most decorated chefs in the world. Known for his French cuisine excellence.

Signature Dish: Lobster Medallions with Caviar and Cream Sauce.

Philosophy: "Food is about emotions, memories, and the people who share it."

Chef Massimo Bottura

Experience: Owner of Osteria Francescana in Modena, Italy, a three-Michelin-star restaurant. Known for modernizing traditional Italian recipes.

Signature Dish: "Oops! I Dropped the Lemon Tart," a deconstructed dessert masterpiece.

Philosophy: "Tradition is never static. It evolves with creativity and emotion."

Chef Baek Jong Won

Experience: A celebrated South Korean chef, restaurateur, and food researcher known for popularizing Korean cuisine globally. He owns multiple restaurant chains and is a beloved TV personality.

Signature Dish: Kimchi Stew (Kimchi-jjigae).

Philosophy: "Good food is not about luxury, but about bringing people together."
